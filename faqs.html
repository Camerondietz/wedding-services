Wedding Websites

How long does it take to build a wedding website?

Most Signature builds go live within 2–3 weeks of the discovery call. We accept content edits all the way up to your wedding week.

Can guests RSVP through the site?

Yes — every package includes a working RSVP form. Submissions are emailed to you and exportable as a CSV guest list. Meal choice and plus-one fields are configurable.

Do I need to buy my own domain?

Optional. We can launch on a subdomain we provide, or help you register a custom domain (typically $12–18/year, paid directly to the registrar).

What happens to the site after the wedding?

Signature includes hosting through your wedding month. Heirloom keeps it live for a full year so guests can revisit photos and thank-you notes. Static archives are also available.

Can you migrate from another platform like The Knot or Zola?

Yes. We import what's useful (registry links, schedule details, guest list) and leave behind the bloat.

Bridesmaids Facial Waxing

Do you only serve clients in Austin?

Our home studio is in Austin. We travel for on-site bookings throughout the metro. A $35 travel fee applies outside city limits.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend 8–12 weeks ahead. Peak-season Saturdays (Mar–Jun, Sep–Nov) often fill 6+ weeks out.

How early should the bride book her wax?

3–5 days before the ceremony is the sweet spot — long enough for any redness to fade, short enough that brows still look freshly shaped.

What if someone in the bridal party is a first-time waxer?

We strongly recommend a patch test 2 weeks ahead. It's free and takes 5 minutes. We can also do a single-area test wax during the same visit.

Is your wax safe for sensitive skin or retinol users?

We use hard wax exclusively on the face and stock a sensitive-skin formula. Anyone using retinol, accutane, or chemical exfoliants should pause those products for the windows we'll share during booking.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes — when 4 or more bridesmaids book the Bridal Trio together, the group rate applies automatically. When 5 or more book, the bride's service is complimentary.
