Frequently asked

Anything else? Email us at hello@example.com.

What's the dress code?

Garden formal. Think long dresses, jumpsuits, suits in earth tones (sage, cream, terracotta). The ceremony and cocktail hour are on grass — flats or block heels recommended.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

Outdoors in a pecan grove. We have a rain plan with a covered pavilion if needed — we'll send out a note that morning if anything changes.

Can I bring a plus-one?

Your invitation lists who's invited. If a plus-one is included, you can add their name during RSVP.

Are kids welcome?

We love your kids — and we're keeping the celebration adults-only with the exception of our nieces and nephew. Thank you for understanding.

Is there parking?

Yes, free parking is on-site. We're also running shuttles from The Driftwood Inn starting at 3:15 PM and back to the hotel until midnight.

What time should I arrive?

Plan to be seated by 4:15 PM. The ceremony starts promptly at 4:30 PM and is about 25 minutes long.

Are you registered anywhere?

Yes — check the registry page for our list (and a charity option if you'd rather give that way).

What about COVID / illness?

Please stay home if you're feeling unwell — we'll celebrate with you another way. We'll have masks available for anyone who'd like one.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We're going unplugged for the ceremony itself — please leave phones and cameras in your bag until we're back down the aisle. Snap away during cocktails and reception!
